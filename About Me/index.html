About Me

Hi I am Andrew.

For over 11 years I have worked as a Middle/High school Mathematics and Computer Studies teacher in the international sector. In 2016 I decided to follow my passion for coding and changed careers. I completed a Master's in Software Engineering at the University of Stirling. Each module, and even my dissertation, were individually graded at a distinction level; giving me an overall distinction and making me the top student of my cohort.

I am passionate about learning and love acquiring new skills. At the moment my interests lie in making mobile applications, using native and cross-platform technologies. Though I have been known to dabble in backend technologies.

I volunteer at Codebase Stirling's biweekly Prewired event, helping to mentor new coders.

I was a technical editor on Daniel Steinberg's latest books on SwiftUI and functional programming, which can be purchased respectively on Gumroad here and here. I really recommend them as they are a fantastic resource.
